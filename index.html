Our Story

Boostic is a rapidly growing sales network comprised of some of the most talented salespeople in the world, across the globe.

We help you to create a connection between sales champions and relevant solutions for the customers they manage, so everybody wins.

Companies can choose between having a referral, a co-management of the account or full account management delegated to the sales champion.

Our Vision

Boostic has listened to the market to reinvent the way products and services are sold, democratizing the opportunities to sell to complex organizations and boost your sales, no matter how the size of your company or location.

We have heard hundreds of salespeople identifying themselves as "facilitators" or "helpers" to their accounts. As a result, we are enabling a win-win ecosystem where sales become a trusted partner for their accounts and thru Boostic the best products are offered for them to find a perfect fit product/service that meets their customer needs.

Out Technology

Boostic is a Saas matchmaking platform between sales and corporations for a perfect fit matchmaking.

There is a qualification process to guarantee the quality of both the companies supplying products/services and the sales champion to maximize success while protecting privacy.

Both the product/service company and the sales champion have to approve the match, boosting success while reducing sales lifecycle and its associated risk and cost.
